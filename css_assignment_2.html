DIFFERENCE BETWEEN PADDINGS, MARGINS AND BORDERS

PADDING is used to create space around the content. It is always inside the borders. We have different properties for setting the padding which is the padding top,
padding bottom, padding right and padding left.

MARGIN is the space around the content, outside of the borders, There are properties for setting each side of the content i.e
margin top, margin bottom, margin left and margin right.

BORDERS are in between the padding and the margin. Border is used
to separate the padding from the margin
